What It Analyzes

Every layer of the email - fully exposed

The detector goes beyond simple header checks. It parses every technical layer, cross-references against VirusTotal, extracts IOCs, and explains every finding in plain language.

Authentication & Header Forensics Validates SPF, DKIM, and DMARC with alignment checks. Detects Reply-To/Return-Path mismatches, display name spoofing, and traces the full received-hop chain with IPs.

URL Inspection Extracts, defangs, and analyzes all URLs from HTML body and text. Checks host reputation, deception indicators, redirect chains, and QR code embedded links from images.
